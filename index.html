Questions before you book

How fast will I start seeing meetings?

Most clients see meetings landing within the first 2 to 3 weeks of launch. Our guarantee is 20 qualified meetings inside your ICP within 30 days of campaigns going live.

What counts as a qualified meeting?

A booked call with a decision-maker who matches your ideal customer profile and has agreed to hear about your offer.

Do I need my own infrastructure?

No. On our all-inclusive plans we set up and manage everything for you, from domains to sending infrastructure. If you already have your own, our service-only plans cost less.

What kind of companies do you work with?

We work with B2B companies, agencies and founders who sell to other businesses. If you have a proven offer and sell to businesses, we can very likely fill your pipeline.

How involved do I need to be?

Very little. We handle strategy, data, copy, outreach and booking. You get a weekly consultation and you show up to the meetings we book.

What if it doesn't work?

Then you do not pay. If we miss 20 qualified meetings in 30 days, you get your money back.
