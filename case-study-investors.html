Investor outreach case study

18 investors and $1.2M raised from cold email in 90 days.

We built a focused outbound engine that turned a limited warm network into a scalable investor pipeline with fast follow-up, tight segmentation, and enough volume to book meetings weeks in advance.

The challenge

The client needed to reach more than 100 qualified investors quickly, but their warm network was limited and traditional outreach was too slow. They needed a repeatable system that could identify the right prospects, manage follow-ups, and turn interest into booked investor calls.

What we built

  • Pulled connections from roughly 300 existing contacts.
  • Created a master pool of 150,000 targeted prospects.
  • Segmented prospects into weekly campaigns of 5,000 people each.
  • Kept campaigns producing 100 to 130 positive replies per week.

How meetings were booked

Every interested reply was handled within 5 minutes. The campaign used an 8-touch follow-up system and maintained a 40 to 50 percent booking rate. Because the volume was so high, investor calls were being booked one month in advance.

The outcome

The system contacted 22,451 leads, sent 44,685 follow-ups, produced 470 positive replies, booked 188 meetings, and helped close 18 investors. Total investment raised reached $1.2M, with a 51x return on the total campaign investment.

Result snapshot

  • Average reply rate: 5.09 percent.
  • Email to positive reply: 1 in 140.
  • Meetings attended: 132.
  • Closed and won: 18 investors.
